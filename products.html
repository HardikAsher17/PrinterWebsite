Ender 6

New to the Ender series is an acrylic enclosure, which, from what we can understand, is an optional upgrade for the Ender 6. A simple mechanical bracket system holds the acrylic walls in place. The top of the machine isn’t covered as the printhead is mounted here. Two front doors give you room to pluck your parts off the print bed once they’re finished.
