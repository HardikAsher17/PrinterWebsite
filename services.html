Services


Prototyping

We develop a prototype and quickly fabricate a scale model using three-dimensional computer aided design (CAD) data. We use "additive layer manufacturing" technology for construction of the part. Prototype images provided by you will be converted into real-world objects!


Education

We help develop tools to aid in education. This technique exposes students to a variety of learning experiences. Using 3D printers promotes development of problem solving skills and skills of persistence and prowess to overcome challenges.


Designing

We help serve designers to serve their design needs. 3D printing helps designers design in a more creative, nimble and cost-effective way. This helps designers to actually see what their products looks like, and what changes need to be made to make the design more appealing.
